Who

My name is Gastón since 1996. They gave it to me in Montevideo, Uruguay, South America, Planet Earth, Solar System, Milky Way, Internet.

I am finishing my studies in visual communication design (LDCV) in Facultad de arquitectura, diseño y urbanismo (FADU), Universidad de la República (UdelaR).

I usually work as graphic designer, but I also explore other visual communication roles, such as photography, the audiovisual universe and web design as experience.

I am interested in learning about drawing, painting, writing, literature, printing methods, book publishing, the art of videogames, football, cinema and music.

Also about communities, groups, cooperatives and any form of groups of people who come together to follow their ideas, motivations or indignations, to transform ways of living.

We come to do that, right?
