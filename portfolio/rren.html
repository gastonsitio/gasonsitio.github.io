RR Etiquetas

RR is a label printing factory with a large number of customers. There we made an incredible design team with Cristian Recoba and sensei Daniel Gandolfo. We took initiative in developing new channels in which a business such as an industrial and commercial printing factory could expand. We always design considering what the art of printing means to the people who works there, what it implies in terms of creative and manual work, from its chain work processes and the responsibility of each person.

Despite commercial inflexibility that traditional industries usually have in Uruguay, we did the best we could with the creative freedom we had. Always debating between business relationships and design work, we often achieved very satisfactory results, and above all, we managed to add a bit of color to the monotonous factory routine.

Labels, brands and products for clients with strict quality controls. We used various materials and finishes for labels. From cold stamping to special inks, varnishes and custom die-cuts.

Advertising product design

Business gift consisting of a box with 2 craft beers. The beer labels and the design of the box were designed under the concept of wishing for tranquility and projection to the future, since first year of pandemic was ending and work was being carried out in constant uncertainty.

The illustrations on the labels show landscapes of Piriápolis and the Sierra de Minas through a selection of warm colors that seeks to evoke that sensation of calm, being also paradigmatic places in our country for those who seek to relax.

World Cup fixture

What would a fixture made by a label printer look like? Following the tradition of the printers in Uruguay, we designed an interactive product between a sticker album and a fixture to celebrate a new World Cup. The product is a double-sided brochure that includes a sheet of stickers with the flags of the participating countries in order to use them to complete the results of the championship.

Institutional and product photography

We took photographs to have material that allows us to develop the company image. Most of these are used on the RR website and in institutional videos.
